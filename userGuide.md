## Managing Your Website

Access the Management UI from the Chatbox header icon.

**Preview Panel**: View live site with responsive design testing across devices.

**Code Panel**: Browse all project files and download source code.

**Settings → General**: Update "The Skool Launchpad" title or logo via VITE_APP_TITLE and VITE_APP_LOGO variables.

**Settings → Domains**: Customize your xxx.manus.space subdomain or connect a custom domain.
